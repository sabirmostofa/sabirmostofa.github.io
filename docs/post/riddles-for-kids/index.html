Riddles for kids

 Posted on September 27, 2020  |  2 minutes  |  

Collected Riddles:

People Buy me to eat but they never eat me, what am I?

Plate

If I have it, I don’t share it. If I share it, I don’t have it. What is it?

Secret

What word begins and ends with an E but only has one letter?

Envelope

What has a neck but no head?

Bottle

Why did the boy bury his flashlight?

Batteries died

Which letter of the alphabet has the most water?

C

What starts with a P, ends with an E and has thousands of letters?

Post Office

Why would a man living in New York not be buried in Chicago?

Because he is still living!

What is the difference between a jeweler and a jailer?

A jeweler sells watches. A jailer watches cells.

What is so delicate that saying its name breaks it?

Silence

Everyone gives different answers to a same question but everyone is right. whats the question?

Whats your name?

I am dark but i dont exist without light, what am I?

Shadow

There are rivers but no water. Thare are mountains but no rock. There are cities but no people. What/where is it?

A map

One man went swimming without cap, he swam freestyle, under the water but his didn't wet his hair. How?

He is bald

Grandpa went out for a walk and it started to rain. He didn’t bring an umbrella or a hat. His clothes got soaked, but not a hair on his head was wet. How is this possible?

He is bald

What’s really easy to get into, and hard to get out of?

Trouble

I am often following you and copying your every move. Yet you can never touch me or catch me. What am I?

A shadow

Why are ghosts bad at lying?

Cause, You can see through them!

If you throw a blue stone into the Red Sea, what will it become?

wet/drown

What has hands, but can’t clap?

Clock

What has legs, but doesn’t walk?

Table

What can you break, even if you never pick it up or touch it?

Promise

What can you catch, but not throw?

Cold

I am always in front of you and never behind you. What am I?

Your future

What kind of band never plays music?

Rubber band

I have many teeth, but I cannot bite. What am I?

A comb
